About Us

In 1979 our founder, Youn Jae Lee, started his business in the great outdoors, making tents in South Korea. Eventually, he moved Zinus into the home and has provided mattresses and furniture for more than 25 million happy customers in over 15 countries around the world. We’re committed to helping everyone bring happiness home. That’s why Zinus products are easy to afford, assemble, and enjoy. We design tiny reminders that life is good.
